The "Hell Week" Protocol

Designate one week every month to absolute zero non-essential spending. Eliminate all dining, leisure, and convenience purchases to drastically widen your savings margin and reset baseline dopamine.

Deployment

IPO & ETF Liquidity Stash

Do not tie up 100% of your capital in scheduled mandates. Maintain an isolated, liquid cash buffer specifically to attack market dips via ETFs or block immediate funds for high-conviction IPOs.

The 50/30/20 Rule

Needs 50% | Wants 30% | Invest 20%

Never let your "Wants" eat into your "Invest" capital. Transfer your investment funds out of your primary account on payday. Treat it as a non-negotiable tax on yourself.
