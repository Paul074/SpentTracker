🪁 Privacy Policy

Kite Finance — Personal Finance Tracker
Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Kite Finance ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile and web application handles your information.

Kite Finance is a privacy-first app. All your financial data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial information on our servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be transparent. Kite Finance does NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial data (transactions, income, expenses, investments)
  • Banking or credit card information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Contact lists or address book
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into Kite Finance (transactions, budgets, categories, credit card info, insurance details, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage technology (localStorage).

This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your data even if we wanted to
  • Your data is not sent to any server, cloud, or third party
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all your data permanently

4. Data You Export Manually

Kite Finance allows you to export your data manually as JSON, CSV, or PDF files for backup purposes. When you choose to export:

  • The exported file is saved to your device's download folder
  • You are fully responsible for where you share or store this file
  • We do not receive a copy of any exported data

5. Permissions We Request

Kite Finance may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage access: Only when you export a backup file — to save it to your device
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback on button taps (optional)
  • Notifications: For optional monthly summary reminders (you can disable anytime)

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive permission.

6. Third-Party Services

Kite Finance does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data-sharing services.

The app is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log anonymous connection data (IP address, browser type) as per their standard server logs. This is beyond our control. You can read GitHub's privacy policy at docs.github.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Kite Finance is not targeted at children under 13. Since we do not collect any user data, no personal information from children is ever transmitted to us. However, we recommend parental supervision for young users.

8. Data Deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. To delete all your data:

  • Open the app → Settings → Data → Reset All Data
  • Or, uninstall the app — this removes all stored data
  • Or, clear your browser storage for the app's domain

There is no server-side account or data to delete, because none exists.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

If we ever introduce features that collect data (such as optional cloud sync or advertisements), we will provide clear notice before such features are activated, and your consent will be required.
